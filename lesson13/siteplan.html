Site Purpose

Here at Scoots Rental we work to serve you. We look forward to your time with us here on our Cozumel Island. Whether you are a local or a tourist we want to encourage you to get around the island with ease, while enjoying our paradise. Our site provides transportation for one individual or five individuals for your convenience. We are ready to help serve you as you travel around our beautiful island. We provide great service through transportation but also through our site. Our site makes reserving your scooter or jeep with ease. Feel free to leave us comment or inquire if anything arises. Have a wonderful day!
