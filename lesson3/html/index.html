Overview

Purpose

Provide a weather website to help the public know what to expect and when to plan their adventures. We provide moment to moment updates giving our users the most current information.

Audience

This site is to encourage to support the outside adventurer. We will provide an up to date daily forecast giving the adventurer opportunities to plan daily or future activites with their peers.
