City of Soda Springs has officially canceled recreational programs through the end of June.

Due to the current COVID-19 health risks the City of Soda Springs has officially canceled recreational programs through the end of June. The City’s decision was not made easily and was based on the Governor’s guidelines for youth activities https://rebound.idaho.gov/…/opening-up-guidlines_rev051420.… This means there will be no City Recreation baseball, softball, t-ball, or coach pitch this year. The City does not feel it can adequately comply with the safety guidelines and provide these programs in the short season that remains. The City hopes our community understands the need to cancel these activities this year for the safety of our kids and the community. Around the 1st part of July the City will start to consider how and if the next set of sports programs can be held including soccer, flag football and volleyball. The City would encourage families to get out and be active taking advantage of the many open spaces that can be utilized by family groups while maintaining the physical distancing recommendations.
